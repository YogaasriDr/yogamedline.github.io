About Online Consultation

Due to Covid-19, many are suffering from various health related issue. Moreover, at this time it is not advicable to get consulted in hospitals too.. To Break this challenging situations, We have brought this new idea of online medical consultation

In this Online medical consultation, You can book the appointment from your home.

Things required for efficient consultation from your side are, good internet connective for video call, or good signal strenght for normal phone call

    Procedures to Book appointment

  • Choose your mode of consultation in "Type of Service" tab.(Video Call/Phone Call) [We recommand video call, would be a better option for remote consultation]
  • Click, your mode of consultation, which will take you to appropriate webpage.
  • Please, kindly fill your details and do the payment within "15 mins".
  • After, completing the above steps, We will fix your appoinment timings and you are ready for the consultation.
  • After the consultation, within 5-10 minutes, we will provide e-prescription through email or whatsapp. With this you can buy medicines.
